My Skills.

Three Sword Style

As a swordsman, my style is like no other. I employ the unique and powerful technique known as "Santoryu," or the three sword style. This technique sets me apart from other swordsmen, as I wield one sword in each hand and another in my mouth. With this combination, I can unleash a barrage of slashes and strikes that can take down even the toughest of opponents.
